**Posted:** 21 May 2026

A new hotfix for Android and Realms is rolling out to address issues found in the latest update. It will become available on each platform as it’s approved, so please allow a little time for it to reach you.

## Fixes:[](https://dev.azure.com/dev-mc/Minecraft/_wiki/wikis/Minecraft.wiki/36037/R26U2-Hotfix-3?anchor=fixes%3A)

- Fixed an issue that caused black squares to appear on the screen when Vibrant Visuals was enabled on certain Android devices ([MCPE-238627](https://bugs.mojang.com/browse/MCPE-238627))
- Fixed several crashes that could occur during gameplay
