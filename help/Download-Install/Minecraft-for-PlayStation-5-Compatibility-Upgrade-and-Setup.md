If you own the PlayStation 4 (PS4) version of Minecraft, you can upgrade to the PlayStation 5 (PS5) version for improved performance. Here’s how to upgrade your PS4 downloaded or disc version to PS5.

## Upgrade from the PS4 download version

If you own the PS4 license for Minecraft on your PlayStation Network (PSN) account, find and download the PS5 version in the PlayStation Store.

1.  Start your PlayStation 5 and sign in to the PSN account that owns a license to Minecraft.
2.  Select **Game Library**.
3.  Go to the **Your Collection** tab.
4.  Find and select **Minecraft**.
5.  Select the PS5 version to go to the store page.
6.  Select **Download**.

## Upgrade from the PS4 disc version

If you own the Minecraft PS4 disc version, insert the PS4 game disc to play the PS5 digital game. You will need to keep the disc inserted to play the game.

1.  Sign in to your existing PSN account on your PS5 or create an account.
2.  Insert the Minecraft for PS4 game disc into your PS5.
3.  Go to the game hub for Minecraft for PS4.
4.  Select the **upgrade offer** on the game hub and review the upgrade offer.
5.  Select **Download** to download content.

## Next steps to finalize your transfer

- **Link your Microsoft account and PSN account**: If you haven’t already, [Link Your Microsoft Account to Your PlayStation Network Account](../Use-or-Link-Microsoft-Accounts/Link-Your-Microsoft-Account-to-Your-PlayStation-Network-Account-in-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition.md). This will let you access cross-platform play and your Minecraft Marketplace purchases from other devices.
- **Authorize Minecraft subscription services**: If you have a Minecraft Realms or Marketplace Pass subscription, [Authorize Minecraft Subscription Services on PlayStation 5](../Marketplace-Realms-Subscriptions/Authorizing-Minecraft-Subscription-Services-on-PlayStation-5.md) as a trusted app to enable automatic renewal of your subscriptions.
- **Transfer your save data**: Move your save data from the cloud with your PlayStation Plus subscription by following the steps to [Manage Minecraft Cloud Saves on PlayStation](../Backup-Restore/Manage-Minecraft-Cloud-Saves-on-PlayStation.md). You can also [Use Realms to Transfer a Minecraft: Bedrock Edition World to Another Device](../Backup-Restore/Use-Realms-to-Transfer-a-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition-World-to-Another-Device.md).
