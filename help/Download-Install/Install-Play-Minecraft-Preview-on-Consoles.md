Minecraft Preview allows you to play an in-development version of Minecraft: Bedrock Edition and test features before theyâ€™re fully released on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox X/S consoles. [Learn About Minecraft Beta and Preview](./Learn-About-Minecraft-Beta-and-Preview.md).

To play Minecraft Preview, you must own the digital version of Minecraft for your platform. Xbox players can also access preview if Minecraft is available to them through [Xbox Game Pass Ultimate](https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass).

## Install Minecraft Preview on PlayStation4/5

1.  Open Minecraft on your PlayStation.
2.  Select **Settings** on the Minecraft Launch screen.
3.  Go to Preview at the bottom of the Settings menu.
4.  Select **Get Preview for PlayStation 4** or **PlayStation 5**.
5.  Select **Download** from the PlayStation Store listing.

## Install Minecraft Preview on Xbox One, X/S

1.  Open the Store and search for **Minecraft Preview**.
2.  Select **Install**.
