Minecraft Preview allows you to play an in-development version of Minecraft: Bedrock Edition and test features before they’re fully released on PlayStation 4 (PS4), PlayStation 5 (PS5), Xbox One, and Xbox X/S consoles. [Learn About Minecraft Beta and Preview](./Learn-About-Minecraft-Beta-and-Preview.md).

To play Minecraft Preview, you must own the digital version of Minecraft for your platform. Xbox players can also access preview if Minecraft is available to them through [Xbox Game Pass Ultimate](https://www.xbox.com/en-US/xbox-game-pass).

## Install Minecraft Preview on PS4/PS5

1.  Open Minecraft on your PlayStation.
2.  Select **Settings **on the Minecraft Launch screen.
3.  Go to Preview at the bottom of the Settings menu.
4.  Select **Get Preview for PlayStation 4** or **PlayStation 5**.
5.  Select **Download** from the PlayStation Store listing.

## Install Minecraft Preview on Xbox One, X/S

1.  Open the Store and search for **Minecraft Preview**.
2.  Select **Install**.

## Play Minecraft Preview on PS4/PS5

The Minecraft Store is not supported on the PlayStation versions of Minecraft Preview. To make purchases, use the live version. Once purchased, resource and behavior packs can be enabled in Minecraft Preview from **Settings** \> **Global Resources**.
