If you encounter a 'Couldn't Create Realm Right Now' error, here are some things to try to get your world going again:

- **Confirm your subscription is active:** Ensure that you do have an active Realms subscription for the platform (such as PlayStation, Xbox , or mobile) you used to start your Realm. Check that your subscription is active, not expired or past due.  See the following resource for your Minecraft Edition for more information:
  - [View your Minecraft: Bedrock Edition Realms Subscription Renewal Date.](../Manage-Realms-Subscriptions/View-a-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition-Realms-Subscription-Renewal-Date.md)
  - [View your Minecraft: Java Edition Realms Subscription Renewal Date ](../Manage-Realms-Subscriptions/View-a-Minecraft-Java-Edition-Realms-Subscription-Renewal-Date.md)
- **Check your internet connection:** A stable connection is required to complete the setup process.  
  - Verify your device is connected to the internet
  - Restart your router if needed
  - Disable VPNs or network filters temporarily
  - Try again on another network if available 
- **Restart Minecraft:** Try logging out of the Microsoft account associated with your game, restarting the game, and then logging back in. This refreshes your profile's authentication and connection with our servers. After restarting the game and logging back in, check to see if the Realm appears. 
- **Try to start a new subscription:** You can try entering the Marketplace and restarting the purchase flow by clicking **Subscribe Now**. You will not need to complete the purchase again, so make sure you do not progress beyond the platform store pop up. This should cause the game to retry creating your Realm. 

If you are encountering payment errors, visit [Troubleshoot Realms Subscription Payment Errors](../Manage-Realms-Subscriptions/Troubleshoot-Realms-Subscription-Payment-Errors.md) for more assistance. 

If you are still encountering an error when creating a Realm, [contact us](https://help.minecraft.net/hc/en-us/requests/new) so we can take a closer look and help complete your Realm setup.
