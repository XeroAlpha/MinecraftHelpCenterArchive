Create a party of up to 15 players and experience easy, group travel between Minecraft: Bedrock multiplayer worlds! Before you create a party, [Learn About Minecraft Parties](./Learn-About-Minecraft-Parties.md) and [Add Friends to Your Friend List in Minecraft: Bedrock Edition](../Multiplayer-Support/Add-Friends-to-Your-Friend-List-in-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition.md).

## How to create a party and invite friends

1.  Launch Minecraft: Bedrock Edition and select **Play**.
2.  Select the **Social** button in the top-right.
3.  Select the **Party** tab (group silhouette).
4.  Select **Create party**.
5.  Select **Open** or **Invite only** for your party’s privacy.
6.  Select the invite button next to the name of your friend. If you need to resend an invite, select the **resend invite** button.

If you don’t see any players, [Add Friends to Your Friend List in Minecraft: Bedrock Edition](../Multiplayer-Support/Add-Friends-to-Your-Friend-List-in-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition.md).

## How to join a party

You can join a party by either accepting an invite or joining an open party from the social menu.

- **To accept an invite**: Select the Social button \> Party tab \> Accept invite
- **To join a party**: Select the Social button \> Party tab \> Join
