The owner of a Minecraft: Bedrock Edition Realm can assign trusted players as **Admins **to help manage their community. Admins can manage players and settings within the Realm, but they can’t manage the subscription or assign new Admins.  To promote a player in your Realm to an Admin, follow these steps:

1.  Open Minecraft and select **Play**.
2.  Select the **Realms **tab.
3.  Select **Realms Hub**.
4.  Select the **Community **tab.
5.  Select** Members **from the sidebar.
6.  Locate the player’s name by scrolling through the Player list or searching for the player using the search bar.
7.  Select the **Options **button** **to the right of the player’s name.
8.  Select **Edit role**.
9.  Under Realms Role, toggle the player role from Player to **Administrator**.
