An error can occur when joining a world if you are using Character Creator items, skins, or Addâ€‘Ons that are incompatible with the world you are trying to join. Use the suggestions below to change skins or settings and try to join your world again.

- **Adjust Trusted Skins Settings:** Using a skin from an unverified source that may be incompatible with the world you are trying to join can cause world connection errors. Open Settings/General and toggle Only Allow Trusted Skins off.
- **Remove Character Creator Items:** Select Edit Character in the Dressing Room and remove any custom items such as Hair, Eyes, Outerwear, or Back Items.
- **Switch to a Default Skin:** Reverting to a classic skin helps confirm whether your current skin is causing the issue. Choose a Classic Skin from the Dressing Room. See [Equip Minecraft Character Creator Items and Skins in the Dressing Room](../Managing-Marketplace-Content/Equip-Minecraft-Character-Creator-Items-and-Skins-in-the-Dressing-Room.md).
- **Check for incompatible Add-Ons:** Visit [Disable Minecraft: Bedrock Edition Add-Ons to Solve Game Issues](../Marketplace-Troubleshooting/Disable-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition-Add-Ons-to-Solve-Game-Issues.md) to learn how to troubleshoot Add-On issues.
