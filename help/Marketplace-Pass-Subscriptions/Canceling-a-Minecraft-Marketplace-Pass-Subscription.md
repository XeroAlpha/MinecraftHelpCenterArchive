**How do I cancel my Marketplace Pass Subscription?**

Your subscription is managed from the store platform where you originally started the subscription. Here’s how to cancel or turn off auto-renewal for each platform:

### Android and Chromebook

1.  Go to [Google Play](https://play.google.com/) and sign into the Google account used to purchase your subscription.
2.  Select **My subscriptions** on the left.
3.  Select the subscription you want to cancel.
4.  Select** Manage **and **Cancel Subscription**.

See [Cancel, pause, or change a subscription on Google Play](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/7018481?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en&oco=0) for more information.

### iOS

1.  Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
2.  Sign into the Apple ID you used when you purchased your subscription.
3.  Select the account name.
4.  Select **Subscriptions.**
5.  Select the subscription that you want to manage.
6.  Select **Cancel Subscription.**

See [If you want to cancel a subscription from Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-us/118428) for more information.

### Nintendo Switch

1.  Open the **Nintendo eShop** from the Nintendo Switch HOME Menu. 
2.  Select the Nintendo Account that purchased the subscription.
3.  Select your user icon in the top-right corner of the screen.
4.  Select **Your Subscriptions** to view a list of in-game passes you are subscribed to.
5.  Select **Turn Off Automatic Renewal** next to the in-game subscription to cancel automatic renewal.

See [How to Cancel Auto-Renewal for an In-Game Subscription](https://en-americas-support.nintendo.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/54285/kw/subscription/p/994/c/251) for more information.

### PlayStation

1.  Sign in to [Account Management](https://www.playstation.com/acct/management).
2.  Select **Subscription** from the menu on the left of the screen.
3.  Select **Turn Off Auto-Renew** next to the subscription you would like to cancel.

See [How to manage subscriptions on PlayStation (US)](https://www.playstation.com/en-us/support/store/cancel-ps-store-subscription/) to manage your subscriptions on your console.

### Windows 10 and above

1.  Go to the [Microsoft Account page](https://account.microsoft.com/services/) and log in to your account.
2.  Find the subscription you want to cancel.
3.  Select **Manage Subscription**.
4.  Update your subscription as needed.

### Xbox

1.  Sign into[ Services & Subscriptions](https://account.microsoft.com/services/) with the account you used when you purchased your subscription.
2.  Find your subscription.
3.  Select **Manage.**
4.  Select **Cancel**.
5.  Follow the instructions on the page to proceed with cancellation.

See [How to cancel your Microsoft subscription](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4522164/microsoft-account-how-to-cancel-your-subscription?fref=services_help_cancelSub) for more information.
