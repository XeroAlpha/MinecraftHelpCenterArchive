Minecraft events and promotions often award capes that you can equip on your Minecraft: **Java Edition** character to show your participation. After a cape is awarded or you [redeem a code](../Redeeming-Gifts-Codes/How-to-Redeem-a-Code-for-In-Game-Downloadable-Content-in-Minecraft.md), it first appears in the dressing room of your Minecraft: **Bedrock Edition** profile. To activate your cape on your **Java Edition** profile, you must sign into **Bedrock Edition** and claim it.

## How to activate a Bedrock Edition Cape in Java Edition

1.  Wait for notification that your cape was granted or redeem your code.
2.  Open the Minecraft Launcher and sign in with your Microsoft account.
3.  Open Minecraft: Bedrock Edition.
4.  Select **Dressing Room** and [locate your item in the appropriate section](../Managing-Marketplace-Content/Equip-Minecraft-Character-Creator-Items-and-Skins-in-the-Dressing-Room.md).
5.  Exit Minecraft: Bedrock Edition and open **Minecraft: Java Edition**.
6.  Select **Skins** and highlight the skin you are currently using.
7.  Select **…** and select **Edit**.
8.  Select your cape and select **Save & Use**.
9.  Close and restart Minecraft: Java Edition.

When you open Minecraft: Java edition again, it should recognize your cape.
