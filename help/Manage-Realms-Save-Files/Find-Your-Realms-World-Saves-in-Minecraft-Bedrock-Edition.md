Automatic and manual backups of your Realms world are stored in Realm storage to help you manage your worlds without losing progress. Here's how to find and manage your saves.

## Access Realm Storage

1.  Launch Minecraft.
2.  Select the **Play **button.
3.  Select the **Realms **tab. 
4.  Select the **Realms Hub **of your realm.
5.  Select the **World **tab.
6.  Select **Saves**.

You have the option to view and manage both Manual and Automatic saves in this menu.

Here are some additional tips:

- **Realms save storage is limited**: If you need to free up space, back up your world to your device storage before you delete data from Realm storage to avoid data loss.
- **Resource packs do not increase your world's storage space:** When you download a saved world, the world and its associated packs are included.
