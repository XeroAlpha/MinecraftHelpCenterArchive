If you have a Minecraft Realms or Marketplace Pass subscription through your PlayStation 5 (PS5), you'll need to authorize Minecraft as a trusted app to automatically renew your subscriptions and avoid cancellation. This also applies if you transfer a subscription from a PlayStation 4 (PS4) to the PS5.

If you do not authorize Minecraft as a trusted app, your Realms and/or Marketplace Pass subscriptions will be cancelled after each monthly billing unless you sign into Minecraft on your PS5. Cancelled Realms and Marketplace subscriptions will prevent you and other players from accessing your Realms and/or Marketplace Pass catalog items.

## How to authorize Minecraft Subscription Services on your PS5

1.  Start Minecraft on your PS5.
2.  Select **Settings**.
3.  Select **Account** under General.
4.  Select **Manage PSN Authorization**.
5.  Select **Accept and Continue**.
