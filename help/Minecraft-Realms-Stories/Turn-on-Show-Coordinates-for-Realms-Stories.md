Realms Stories will keep a record of some of the biggest accomplishments that happen within your Realm. When you enable Realm Event Coordinates in your Realm world settings, Realms Stories will record the location of certain Realm Events when they appear on your story feed. Here's how Realm owners can enable Realm Event Coordinates.

## How to turn on Realm Event Coordinates from the Play screen

1.  Start Minecraft.
2.  Select **Play.**
3.  Select **Realms** on the Play screen.
4.  Select the Realm you want to update and select **Stories**.
5.  Select **Stories Settings** from the top right of your screen. 
6.  Select the **Realm Event Coordinates** toggle to **ON**.

## How to turn on Realm Events Coordinates within your Realm world

1.  Activate the Pause screen while playing in your Realm.
2.  Select **Realm Stories** from the Pause screen.
3.  Select **Stories Settings** from the top right of your screen Select the **Realm Event Coordinates** toggle to **ON**.

**Note**: Not all Realm Events will include an accompanying set of coordinates. If you notice one story not showing where it happened, it may have been because that event doesn’t include the location.\
