Minecraft is available on most devices, so you can always keep on building! Here’s a list of compatible editions and platforms.

## Minecraft: Java Edition

Minecraft Java Edition and Minecraft: Bedrock Edition are sold as a bundle on PC. Here are the [Differences Between Minecraft: Java Edition and Minecraft: Bedrock Edition.](../Buying-Minecraft-Games/Differences-Between-Minecraft-Java-Edition-and-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition.md)

- Platform and store: Windows, Mac, Linux through [minecraft.net](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/store/minecraft-deluxe-collection-pc?tabs=%7B%22details%22%3A0%7D)

## Minecraft: Bedrock Edition

Minecraft Java Edition and Minecraft: Bedrock Edition are sold as a bundle on PC. Here are the [Differences Between Minecraft: Java Edition and Minecraft: Bedrock Edition](../Buying-Minecraft-Games/Differences-Between-Minecraft-Java-Edition-and-Minecraft-Bedrock-Edition.md).

Platform and store:

- Windows 10 (and above), VR Headsets: [minecraft.net](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/store/minecraft-deluxe-collection-pc?tabs=%7B%22details%22%3A0%7D)
- Apple iOS: [Minecraft: Dream it, Build it! App - App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/minecraft-dream-it-build-it/id479516143)
- Android and Chromebook: [Minecraft: Dream it, Build it! - Apps on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mojang.minecraftpe&utm_source=mcnet&pli=1)
- Amazon phones and tablets: [Minecraft - App on Amazon Appstore](https://www.amazon.com/Mojang-Minecraft-Pocket-Edition/dp/B00992CF6W)
- Xbox One, Xbox One X\|S, Xbox Series X\|S: [Xbox Store and Microsoft Store](https://www.xbox.com/en-US/games/store/minecraft/9MVXMVT8ZKWC)
- Nintendo Switch: [Minecraft for Nintendo Switch - Nintendo Official Site](https://www.nintendo.com/us/store/products/minecraft-106679/)†
- PlayStation 4/5: [Minecraft \| PlayStation (US)](https://www.playstation.com/en-us/games/minecraft/)

## Minecraft Classic

The original, free-to-play version of Minecraft!

- Platform and store: Windows, Mac, Linux through [classic.minecraft.net](https://classic.minecraft.net/)

## Minecraft: China Version

Only available in China.

- Platform and store: Windows, Android devices, Apple devices through [mc.163.com](https://mc.163.com/)

## Minecraft: Education

Developed for schools, camps and clubs, with features to create and deliver lessons for all subjects.

- Platform and store: Windows, iPad, Mac, Chromebook through [education.minecraft.net](https://education.minecraft.net/en-us/get-started/download)

## Minecraft: PI Edition

Designed for teaching and tinkering.

- Platform and store: Raspberry Pi and [pi.minecraft.net](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/edition/pi)

† The Super Mario Mash-Up is exclusive to the Nintendo Switch version and only playable in single-player, split-screen, local network, and over Nintendo Switch Online.
