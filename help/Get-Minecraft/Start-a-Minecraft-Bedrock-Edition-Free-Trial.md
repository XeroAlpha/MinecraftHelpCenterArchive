You may be able to try Minecraft: Bedrock Edition before you buy the game. Free trials of Minecraft are available from the store for your gaming platform. Go to [Minecraft Free Trial for Different Devices](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/free-trial) to find supported platform download links.

Here’s what you can expect from the Minecraft: Bedrock Edition Free Trial:

- **Limited availability**: Free trials may not be available for your platform or region.
- **Account sign-in required**: See [Accounts Required to Play Minecraft](../Set-Up-Your-Account/Accounts-Required-to-Play-Minecraft.md). On PC platforms, the Demo is available after you sign in to the launcher if you do not own Minecraft.
- **Limited play time**: The Free Trial version allows you to play for 90 minutes, about four and a half days of in-game time.
- **Limited game modes**: You can play in single-player, Survival mode. Commands are disabled and multiplayer and Realms games are not available.

If you enjoyed the Minecraft trial, [buy Minecraft](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/store/minecraft-deluxe-collection-pc) to unlock the full game.
